Our Story

Locally owned and operated since 1989

Wholesale Siding Supply, Inc. is a locally owned and operated business founded in 1989 in Roanoke, Virginia. We take pride in distributing exterior products for building professionals, and within two years of opening we had already grown to expand our product line.

Over the years we've expanded our product lines and our reach. Today we serve an expansive delivery area across Virginia and West Virginia, with both in-stock and special-order selections for every phase of the job.

  • Extensive in-stock and special-order selection
  • Materials fully supported by major manufacturers
  • Expansive delivery area across Virginia & West Virginia

Made in-house

We manufacture our own gutter coil

In 2008 we opened a dedicated facility to produce our own gutter coil and accessories. Making them ourselves gives us direct control over availability, color and quality, so the material you need is ready when the job calls for it.

Aluminum and steel profiles in a range of widths and colors, plus the downspouts, elbows, hangers and coil stock to finish the run.
