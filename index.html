About Us

Three decades of exterior expertise

Wholesale Siding Supply, Inc. is a locally owned and operated business founded in 1989 in Roanoke, Virginia. We take pride in distributing exterior products for building professionals — and within two years of opening, we'd already grown to expand our product line.

In 2008 we opened a new facility to more efficiently produce our own gutter accessories. Today we serve an expansive delivery area across Virginia and West Virginia, with both in-stock and special-order selections.
