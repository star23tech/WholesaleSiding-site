What sets us apart

01

Premium manufacturer brands

We stock and special-order from the manufacturers contractors trust: StyleCrest, Alside, James Hardie, LP SmartSide, ABC, Gentek, StoneCraft, and more.

02

Contractor pricing on every order

We're a wholesale supplier, which means professional pricing for contractors, remodelers, and production builders regardless of order volume.

Our own gutter facility means we produce coil and accessories on-site, giving us more control over quality, availability, and lead times than a typical distributor.

About Us

A trusted name in exterior building products

"We're serious about service."

Wholesale Siding Supply, Inc. was founded in 1989 in Roanoke, Virginia. For more than three decades, we've supplied building professionals across Virginia and West Virginia with quality exterior products from the manufacturers they know and trust.

We're a locally owned, family-operated wholesale supplier. That means you'll work with people who know the products, know the region, and are focused on making sure you have what you need for your project.

From our in-house gutter production facility to our relationships with leading manufacturers, we're built to serve contractors, builders, and remodelers.
