Why Choose Us

What sets us apart

Building professionals across Virginia and West Virginia have relied on us since 1989. Here's why they keep coming back.

01

Premium manufacturer brands

We stock and special-order from the manufacturers contractors trust: StyleCrest, Alside, James Hardie, LP SmartSide, ABC, Gentek, StoneCraft, and more.

02

Contractor pricing on every order

We're a wholesale supplier, which means professional pricing for contractors, remodelers, and production builders regardless of order volume.

03

In-house gutter production

Our own gutter facility means we produce coil and accessories on-site, giving us more control over quality and availability than a typical distributor.

04

Deep in-stock inventory

Extensive stock across siding, windows, doors, decking, gutters, metal roofing, and trim. When we don't have it, we'll special-order from our manufacturer partners.

05

Regional delivery coverage

Serving Virginia and West Virginia with delivery to job sites across the region. Call ahead to confirm scheduling and routing for your project.

06

Locally owned since 1989

Not a big-box chain or national distributor. A family-owned business with more than three decades of relationships and expertise in this region.
